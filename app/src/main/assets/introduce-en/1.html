APP operation

(1)Registration：

Pls click “register” on the login page when this is your first visit.

Pls input your E-mail address, create the password, accept the 《User Agreement》, finally pls click register to do registration.

After then, User can login the Lite Guardian by this E-mail address and password.

(2)Login：

After e-mail verification, pls login the Lite Guardian by this E-mail address and password.

(3)Account security：

Forget password：If you forget the password, pls click "forget password" on the login page, the e-mail change link will be sent to your registered e-mail address.
