HT-990 Senior Watch

Watch Introduction

① Telling time/Voice chatting functions

Short press,the watch will tell you time;

Long press,the watch will enter"talk"interface,Long press again to record,release to send the voice message to the APP.

② Power on/Power off/SOS/Answer the phone button

Long press to power on/off the watch;

SOS button：after turning on the watch,long pressin 3 seconds, the watch will execute SOS function;

Short press the button to answer the phone when the call.

③ Home/return key（touch key）

④ Volume adjust button/page up/Quickly call

Volume adjust：Under main screen interface,short press this button ,directly enter the volume adjustment interface,if short press again,you can increase the volume.

Page up: Under phone book interface,short press this button to check previous phone number.

Quickly call: under main screen interface,long press this button to call the first contact.

⑤ Volume adjust button/page down/Quickly call

Volume adjust:under volume adjustment interface,short press this button to decrease the volume.

Page down:under phone book interface ,short press this button to check the next phone number.

Quickly call:under main screen interface ,long press this button to call the second contact.

Bind the watch with the APP

1.The watch only support GSM/GPRS network,Please use the standard Nano-SIM card. Open the cover on the back of watch,correctly insert the Nano-SIM card,install the back cover. Long press "on/off " key to turn on the watch.

2.Start the APP: Lite Guardian. Register new account or login existing account, choose "Senior Watch" to bind the watch.

3.Scan the QR code on the rear cover or enter the watch ID number to bind the device.

4.After binding, you can check and set the watch function from mobile APP.

Functions and instruction

1.Check the real-time location by the APP.

2.Time calibration: please choose your local time zone in the "Watch time and language" interface on the APP.

3.Phone book: add contacts via the APP and save,contact book on watch would be synchronized accordingly, and the initial 3 contacts would be default SOS emergency contact person.

4.SOS :When the device is on line normally, press SOS button for 3S, it will start SOS function, if no one answers, it will dial circularly for two rounds to the three emergency contacts; Any of the three em ergency contacts answer the SOS phone, it will stop calling circularly; It will send alert message to the three SOS emergency contacts in the same time for help.

5.Phone call: go to contacts book to choose contacts, click on call icon to make phone calls.

6.Call firewall function:only the contacts in the phone book can call the watch.

7.Medication reminder:can set three alarm clock to remind by the APP.

8.Health monitorin:support pedometer function,sedentary reminder and heart-rate detection function.

9.Instant chat: it enables app to send voice and text message to watch, however watch can only send voice message to the APP.

10.Language setting: please choose the language in the "Watch time and language" interface on mobile APP.

11.It can also set from mobile app to disable the watch for special periods, to set alarm, to find the watch, to power off the watch remotely, to check the historical track ,etc.

APN setting

Please set the APN firstly,or it may unable to obtain positioning data.

How to set APN: APP-Me-Advanced Setting-APN Setting Fill in APN (based on SIM card provider's APN parameter) -then automatically generate a SMS command after you submit, text this message to the watch SIM card phone number.

Notes: Ensure the GSM signal on the watch is normal before setting APN,and make sure the SIM card actives the data and SMS function.

Charging instruction

Please use the special charging cable in the package to charge, the device supports any popular cell phone charger,we do not supply charger for environment friendly purpose.Please select a suitable charger on your hand for charging.

FAQ

1.Why can't I power on the device?

　A:The battery on your device may be running low during long-time transportation, please charge and try again.

　A:Please check whether the charger and cable is properly connected with the watch.

3.Fail to scan QR code?

　A:Please adjust distance between camera and QR code;or try again in a better place with enough light.
