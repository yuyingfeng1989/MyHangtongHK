Main functions

(1)Real-time position：

User can check the device's real-time location information by this APP；Click the location information to display/hide the exact location and time.

(2)Geo-fence：

User can set the geo-fence by APP, when the device exit the geo-fence, device will send alert to contact mobile no. and APP.

(3)History trace：

Select the Home—>history trace，pls select the "start time""end time" and date, finally click search to check history trace.

(4)Family member：

one device could support up to 4 accounts（1 main account +3 sub accounts） to check and management at the same time from mobile APP.

(5)Multi-devices tracking：

one account could support up to 50 devices to check and management from mobile APP.

(6)Alert：

You can check history alert record in one month according to alert types.

(7)Discover：

It supports to locate shops in the surroundings.

(8)Equipment information：

Input /edit the Equipment information：Select the Me—>Equipment information，Click the item to modify, after input or edit, pls click submit to save information.

(9)Walk the dog：

It supports to check the past 1 month activity record to walk your dog.

(10)Vehicle detection：

It could detect vehicle problems by installing the device.

(11)Safe driving：

It could check your score record on safe driving.

(12)Economic driving：

It could check your score record on economic driving.

(13)Setting：

Including the main parameters: frequency setting, positioning tracker alarm settings, restore factory settings etc..
